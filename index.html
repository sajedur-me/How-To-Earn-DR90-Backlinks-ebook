Editorial link: What's that?

First, we must define an editorial link and what differentiates it from other backlink types.

According to Ahrefs, editorial links are organic backlinks. They're not traded, asked, or paid for, so you’re not violating Google’s spam policies by acquiring these links. The fact that editorial links are earned automatically makes them the safest and most valuable backlinks.

However, getting editorial links isn’t easy. You must create good, informative, and valuable content to get this link type for your website. Below are four types of content you must publish on your site for it to attract editorial links:
