Editorial link: What's that?

First, we must define an editorial link and what differentiates it from other backlink types.

According to Ahrefs, editorial links are organic backlinks. They're not traded, asked, or paid for, so you’re not violating Google’s spam policies by acquiring these links. The fact that editorial links are earned automatically makes them the safest and most valuable backlinks.

However, getting editorial links isn’t easy. You must create good, informative, and valuable content to get this link type for your website. Below are four types of content you must publish on your site for it to attract editorial links:

3.1.1: Researching Information Gaps 8

Researching Information Gaps

When we started researching content ideas for our client, we wanted to cover topics that hadn’t been discussed extensively online by other sites. Our goal is to publish content journalists would want to link to and use as a resource in their reports.

And since AI is all the rage nowadays, WriterBuddy is in the prime position to discuss AI-based reports or stats. Initially, we came up with a few topics and what each covers:

The state of AI in the world: Tracks the progress of AI research and development and the adoption of AI by businesses and governments. It could also look at the potential impact of AI on society, both positive and negative.

The future of AI: Explores the potential future of AI, including its potential to revolutionize many industries and aspects of our lives. It could also look at the challenges that must be addressed to ensure that AI is used for good.

Selecting a Topic

With these topics, we decided to create a data-based report that explains the current state of AI. And we found these questions.

  • What are the latest advances in AI research and development?

  • What challenges must be addressed to ensure that AI is used for good?
  • What is the future of AI, and how could it revolutionize many industries and aspects of our lives?

Our team finally set out to explore these questions and went through AI-related studies, whitepapers, and reports. In addition, we paid close attention to tech journalism to identify a knowledge gap in the AI industry.
